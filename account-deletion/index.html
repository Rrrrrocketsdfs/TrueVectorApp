True Vector: Bridge Trainer

How to request deletion

  1. Open the True Vector profile or account screen if you still have app access.
  2. Copy your Account code, Account ID, or UID if it is shown. If you no longer have app access, you can still submit the request without reinstalling the app.
  3. Send an email to truevectorapp@protonmail.com with the subject Account deletion request - True Vector: Bridge Trainer.
  4. Include your account identifier if available and a contact email for confirmation. If you do not know the identifier, include any details that can help locate the account, such as profile name, linked Google email, approximate account creation date, or recent subscription details.
  5. Deletion requests are processed within 30 days after we receive enough information to identify the account.

Data that will be deleted

  • Profile information and app account identifiers.
  • Google identity link, authentication records, device sessions, and push tokens.
  • Profile avatar and related profile media managed by the app.
  • Training progress, session results, skill state, scores, wallet balances, and internal currency records.
  • Messages, conversation data, Tavern posts, comments, votes, pins, and related app community actions associated with the account.
  • Notifications, user inbox state, union memberships, invitations, applications, and app premium entitlement records stored in the app database.

Data that may be retained

  • A minimal deletion or audit record may be retained for up to 180 days to document that the request was processed.
  • Moderation, abuse prevention, fraud prevention, and security records may be retained for up to 180 days where needed to protect the service and users.
  • Billing, subscription, payment provider, legal, tax, or accounting records may be retained as required by Google Play, RevenueCat, payment providers, or applicable law.
  • Aggregated analytics and crash data that is no longer tied to the app account may be retained.

Google Play subscription note

Deleting your True Vector: Bridge Trainer app account does not cancel an active Google Play subscription. Subscription cancellation is handled in Google Play account settings.
