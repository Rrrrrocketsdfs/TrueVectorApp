True Vector: Marine Radar Simulator & Seafarer Hub

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-07-07

True Vector operates True Vector: Marine Radar Simulator & Seafarer Hub. Contact us at truevectorapp@protonmail.com.

The app is anonymous-first. Google Sign-In is optional and used only to link or restore an account.

Data we collect

  • Account/profile: anonymous UID or account code, optional Google identity and email, display name, country, fleet, division, navigator class, avatar and privacy settings.
  • Device/app: install ID, public key, app version/build, locale, notification token when notifications are enabled, and standard server logs such as IP address and user agent.
  • Training: progress, scores, session results, route/scenario state, learning cards and article reactions.
  • Community: posts, comments, votes, messages, unions, invitations, applications, moderation reports and timestamps.
  • Purchases: Google Play Billing and RevenueCat product, transaction and entitlement data. We do not receive or store payment card details.
  • Optional diagnostics: analytics, crash reports and Remote Config data only when enabled by app settings or platform consent controls.
  • Microphone: voice features may use the microphone for speech or VHF-style training. The app backend does not store audio recordings.

How we use data

We use data to create and restore accounts, sync progress, show rankings, run community features, deliver enabled notifications, verify purchases, unlock premium entitlements, protect the service, process reports, improve content and answer support or deletion requests.

Sharing

We do not sell personal data and do not use advertising identifiers for ad targeting. Data may be processed by hosting providers, Google services, Firebase, Play Billing, Remote Config and RevenueCat under their own terms.

Retention and deletion

We keep account, training and community data while your account is active or while needed to provide the service. Deletion requests are handled within 30 days. Backups, security logs, fraud-prevention, legal, revenue or payment records may remain for a limited period, normally up to 90 days for logs/backups.

Your choices

You can edit profile fields, disable notifications, disable optional analytics/crash collection in Settings, and request deletion by emailing your UID/account code or linked Google email to truevectorapp@protonmail.com. Google Play subscription cancellation is managed separately in Google Play.
